User Guide

*Steps for Sign Up*

For New User:

1. On the Landing page click on "Signup" tab. Fill all the required details and click on the "Submit" button.

*Steps for Log In*

After Signing Up:

1. Click on the "Login tab" and Enter your "E-mail Id and Password" for Loging in.
2. After Log in you will be able to see the "Home Page" with your "Name and Photo" and the unfilled tabs.
3. Profile Tab: This Tab will tell you what information has been filled and what information is remaining to fill.
4. Verify E-mail Tab: In this Tab you have to enter your "Registered E-mail Id" and click Enter , then an "OTP" will be sent to your Email-Id.
Type the "OTP" and click on "Submit" button.
5. Record Tab: Click on this Tab and fill all your "Correct Health Details" and then click Submit.
6. After all the details are verified with the help of the "Documents" you provide a "Health Id" will be generated. This "Health Id" will be seen in your "Profile Tab".
7. After the "Health Id" is generated you will be able to view your "Health Card" by clicking on the "View Card Tab". This Health Card can be kept as a Soft Copy.
8. The logout Button is on the "Top Right Corner".
9. After Loging Out ,you cant go back , you have to login again.
